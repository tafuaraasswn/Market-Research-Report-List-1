<p><p>The particle board furniture market includes several types tailored for various applications. Laminated particle board features a surface layer that enhances durability and aesthetics. Cement bonded particle board is highly moisture-resistant, suitable for outdoor or high-humidity environments. Melamine particle board, coated with melamine resin, provides scratch and heat resistance, making it popular for kitchen and office furniture. Each type serves specific consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
