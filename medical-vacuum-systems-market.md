<p><strong>The Medical Vacuum Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Claw Pump Techology</li><li>Dry Rotary Vane Technology</li><li>Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical vacuum systems market encompasses three main technologies: </p><p>1. **Dry Claw Pump Technology**: Utilizes two opposing claws for gas compression, offering oil-free operation and low maintenance, ideal for cleanroom environments.</p><p>2. **Dry Rotary Vane Technology**: Employs rotating vanes in a chamber, providing high efficiency and reliability without oil, suitable for applications requiring contamination-free vacuum.</p><p>3. **Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology**: Involves rotating vanes submerged in oil for lubrication and sealing, delivering high vacuum levels but requiring careful maintenance to manage oil changes and contamination. Each technology meets specific medical needs and regulatory standards.</p></p>
