<p><strong>The Biological Wastewater Treatments Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aerobic</li><li>Anaerobic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biological wastewater treatment systems are categorized mainly into aerobic and anaerobic processes. Aerobic systems utilize oxygen to facilitate the breakdown of organic matter by bacteria, resulting in less sludge production and efficient nutrient removal. Anaerobic systems, on the other hand, operate without oxygen, relying on bacteria to decompose organic material, producing biogas as a byproduct, which can be harnessed for energy. Both systems are crucial for sustainable wastewater management, addressing environmental concerns while promoting resource recovery.</p></p>
