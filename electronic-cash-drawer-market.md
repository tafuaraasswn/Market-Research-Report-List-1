<p><p>The electronic cash drawer market is divided into two main types: stationary and portable. Stationary cash drawers are designed for fixed retail setups, securely integrating with point-of-sale systems, ideal for high-volume transactions. They offer robust security and are typically placed on counters. In contrast, portable cash drawers are lightweight and easily transferable, suited for mobile businesses or temporary setups. They facilitate transactions in various locations, providing flexibility while still ensuring efficient cash management and security.</p></p>
