<p><strong>The Waterjet Cutting Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Abrasive Waterjet</li><li>Pure Waterjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterjet cutting head market is primarily divided into two types: abrasive waterjet and pure waterjet. Abrasive waterjets incorporate an abrasive material, enhancing their cutting capabilities for harder materials like metals and ceramics, making them suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, pure waterjets utilize only high-pressure water, ideal for softer materials such as rubber, food, and certain plastics. Both types are valued for their precision and versatility, catering to diverse industries seeking efficient cutting solutions without generating heat-affected zones.</p></p>
