<p><strong>The Rangefinders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Rangefinder</li><li>Acoustic Rangefinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rangefinder market comprises primarily two types: photoelectric and acoustic rangefinders. Photoelectric rangefinders utilize light, typically laser beams, to measure distances by calculating the time it takes for the light to return after bouncing off an object. They offer high precision and are commonly used in surveying and military applications. Acoustic rangefinders, on the other hand, use sound waves to determine distance by measuring the time it takes for sound to travel to an object and back, often employed in underwater and atmospheric measurements.</p></p>
