<p><p>The Calcium Carbonate Filler market comprises three main types: Heavy Calcium Carbonate, Light Calcium Carbonate, and Crystal Calcium Carbonate. Heavy Calcium Carbonate, also known as ground calcium carbonate (GCC), is used for its bulk and opacity in applications like paints and plastics. Light Calcium Carbonate, or precipitated calcium carbonate (PCC), offers finer particle sizes and suitable for applications requiring brightness and smoothness. Crystal Calcium Carbonate, distinguished by its pure crystalline form, is often utilized in specialized industries like pharmaceuticals and food additives.</p></p>
