<p><p>The RF circulators market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Coaxial Circulators** are designed for high-frequency applications, offering low loss and wide bandwidth. </p><p>2. **Drop-in Circulators** are compact components that can be easily integrated into existing circuits. </p><p>3. **Surface Mount Circulators** offer a smaller footprint for integration into PCB designs, ideal for space-constrained applications. </p><p>4. **Microstrip Circulators** utilize microstrip technology for planar designs, providing efficient performance in various RF applications. Each type caters to specific needs in telecommunications, radar, and other RF systems.</p></p>
