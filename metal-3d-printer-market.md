<p><strong>The Metal 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal 3D printer market is primarily categorized into Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to fuse metal powders layer by layer, enabling the production of complex geometries with excellent mechanical properties. In contrast, EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powders, offering faster build times and improved energy efficiency. Both technologies cater to industries like aerospace, automotive, and medical where precision and material performance are critical.</p></p>
