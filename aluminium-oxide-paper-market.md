<p><p>The Aluminium Oxide Paper market is categorized into various types based on manufacturing methods. The Slurry Method involves mixing aluminium oxide with water to form a paste, which is then applied to paper. The Dipping Method entails immersing paper in a solution of aluminium oxide, allowing for even coating. The Sol-gel Method uses a chemical solution that transforms into a solid gel, enhancing adhesion. Other methods may include spray coating or electrostatic deposition, diversifying production techniques and applications.</p></p>
