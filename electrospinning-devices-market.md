<p><ul><li>Coaxial Electrospinning</li><li>Emulsion Electrospinning</li><li>Melt Electrospinning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrospinning devices market encompasses various techniques for producing nanofibers, each serving distinct applications. Coaxial electrospinning involves two fluids, creating core-shell fibers for drug delivery or filtration. Emulsion electrospinning utilizes dispersed phases to produce fibers with unique properties, suited for encapsulating bioactive compounds. Melt electrospinning, on the other hand, processes polymer melts to fabricate fibers, ideal for textiles and biomedical applications. Each method offers versatility in fabricating advanced materials across multiple industries, including healthcare and environmental sectors.</p></p>
