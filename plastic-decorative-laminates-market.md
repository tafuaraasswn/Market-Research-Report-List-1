<p><p>The Plastic Decorative Laminates market is divided into two main types: Low Pressure Plastic Decorative Laminates (LPL) and High Pressure Plastic Decorative Laminates (HPL). LPL is lighter and often used for interior applications, providing a variety of textures and colors at a more affordable price. In contrast, HPL is manufactured under high pressure, making it more durable and resistant to wear, heat, and moisture, suitable for demanding environments. Both types are widely used in furniture, cabinetry, and wall coverings.</p></p>
