<p><p>The rat model market comprises various types tailored for specific research needs. Knockout rats lack specific genes for studying gene functions. Outbred rats offer genetic diversity, ideal for general research. Inbred rats provide uniformity, ensuring consistency in experiments. Hybrid rats combine traits from different strains for specialized studies. Immunodeficient rats are used in research requiring human tissue or tumor studies. Conditioned rats are trained for behavioral studies, providing insights into neurological and psychological conditions. Each type serves unique research purposes.</p></p>
