<p><p>A mechanical paver is a construction machine used primarily for laying asphalt on roadways, parking lots, and other surfaces. The advantages of mechanical pavers include increased efficiency, improved surface quality, and reduced labor costs compared to manual paving methods.</p><p>The Mechanical Paver Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising demand for infrastructure development and urbanization worldwide.
