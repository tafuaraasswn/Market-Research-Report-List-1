<p><p>The Needle-Free IV Connectors market can be categorized into three types based on their performance and usage: positive, negative, and neutral. Positive connectors actively prevent contamination and reflux, promoting safety and reducing infection risks. Negative connectors prevent backflow but may not offer the same level of protection against pathogens. Neutral connectors balance safety and usability, providing adequate protection without hindering fluid flow. Understanding these types helps healthcare providers select the appropriate connector, enhancing patient safety and treatment efficacy.</p></p>
