<p><p>The High Precision Optical Fiber Fusion Splicer market is segmented into two main types: Single-Mode Fiber and Multimode Fiber. Single-Mode Fiber splicers are designed for fibers that transmit a single light mode, ideal for long-distance communication due to lower attenuation and higher bandwidth capabilities. In contrast, Multimode Fiber splicers cater to fibers that allow multiple light modes, typically used for shorter distances in data centers and local area networks. Both types are vital for enhancing connectivity and communication efficiency.</p></p>
