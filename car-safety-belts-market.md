<p><p>The car safety belts market consists of various types designed to enhance occupant safety in vehicles. Three-point safety belts, which include a shoulder strap, are standard in most vehicles today, providing greater protection by distributing crash forces. Four-point safety belts, often used in racing, offer enhanced stability with two shoulder and two lap straps. Other safety belt types encompass specialized systems like harnesses and child safety seats.</p></p>
