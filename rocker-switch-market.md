<p><strong>The Rocker Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-pole Rocker Switch</li><li>Multipole Rocker Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rocker switch market is primarily divided into two types: single-pole and multipole rocker switches. Single-pole rocker switches control one circuit, making them ideal for simple applications, such as household lighting. Conversely, multipole rocker switches can control multiple circuits simultaneously, allowing for more complex configurations in industrial or commercial settings. Both types are widely used in various devices and appliances, with preferences varying based on application requirements, space constraints, and desired functionality.</p></p>
