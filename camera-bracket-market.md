<p><p>The camera bracket market can be categorized into three primary types: wall-mounted, floor-standing, and ceiling-mounted brackets. Wall-mounted brackets are designed for fixed installations, offering stable support for cameras in various environments, particularly in surveillance. Floor-standing brackets provide portable solutions, enabling easy repositioning and flexibility in camera placement for events or temporary setups. Ceiling-mounted brackets, on the other hand, facilitate optimal overhead viewing, making them ideal for areas requiring comprehensive surveillance coverage and minimizing blind spots.</p></p>
