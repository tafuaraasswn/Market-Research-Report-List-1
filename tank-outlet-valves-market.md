<p><ul><li>Single Seat</li><li>Double Seat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tank outlet valves market is primarily classified into two types: single seat and double seat valves. Single seat valves feature a simplified design with one sealing surface, typically providing reliable performance in low-pressure applications. In contrast, double seat valves consist of two sealing surfaces, which allow for precise control and better flow regulation, making them suitable for high-pressure and critical applications. Each type serves distinct needs in various industries, ensuring effective fluid management in storage tanks.</p></p>
