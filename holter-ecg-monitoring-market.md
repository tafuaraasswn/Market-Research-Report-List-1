<p><p>Holter ECG monitoring is a continuous method of recording the electrical activity of the heart over a 24 to 48-hour period. The market for Holter ECG monitoring has been experiencing significant growth due to increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, technological advancements, and a rising geriatric population. </p><p>Innovative developments in wearable technology and telehealth solutions are enhancing patient experience and accessibility.
