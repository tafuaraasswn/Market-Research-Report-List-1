<p><p>The Inline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) market is categorized into two main types: desktop and freestanding systems. Desktop AOI systems are compact, designed for integration within smaller production lines, ideal for smaller manufacturers or limited space environments. In contrast, freestanding AOI systems are larger, independent units that provide greater flexibility and higher throughput, suitable for larger manufacturing operations. Both types enhance quality control processes by detecting defects in printed circuit boards and electronic assemblies, ensuring reliable product performance.</p></p>
