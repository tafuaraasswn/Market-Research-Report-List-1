<p><p>The agricultural water pump market includes several types of pumps tailored for various irrigation needs. Submersible pumps operate underwater to efficiently draw out water from wells or boreholes. Self-priming pumps are designed to quickly remove air and start pumping without needing manual intervention, making them ideal for surface water applications. Vortex pumps handle dirty water with solids by creating a vortex, preventing clogging. Other market types encompass specialized and niche pumps catering to specific agricultural requirements, enhancing irrigation efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
