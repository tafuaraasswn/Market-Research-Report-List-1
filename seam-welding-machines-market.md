<p><p>Seam welding machines come in three main types: automatic, manual, and semi-automatic. Automatic seam welding machines operate independently, utilizing advanced technology to achieve high precision and efficiency in high-volume production. Manual seam welding machines require operators to control the process, offering flexibility for small-scale operations or custom work. Semi-automatic seam welding machines combine both automated and manual features, allowing for greater control while still improving efficiency, making them suitable for medium production levels and varied applications.</p></p>
