<p><p>The pacemaker devices market is categorized into two primary types: external pacemaker devices and implantable pacemaker devices. External pacemakers are temporary solutions used for short-term cardiac support, typically during surgical procedures or emergencies, providing immediate rhythm control. In contrast, implantable pacemaker devices are surgically placed within the body for long-term management of chronic heart conditions, helping to maintain a regular heartbeat and improve the patient's quality of life. Both types play crucial roles in cardiac care and management.</p></p>
