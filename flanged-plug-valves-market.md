<p><p>Flanged plug valves are specialized valves used in various industrial applications, differentiated mainly by their port designs. Full port valves allow for maximum flow and minimal pressure drop, making them ideal for applications requiring high capacity. Standard port valves have a smaller opening, balancing flow and pressure control, suitable for general use. Round port valves feature a circular opening that optimizes flow dynamics, often used in situations where turbulence reduction is essential. Each type caters to specific operational needs and conditions.</p></p>
