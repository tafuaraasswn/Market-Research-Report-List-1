<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PU Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Water-borne</li><li>High Solids</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PU coating market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Solvent-borne** coatings use organic solvents for application, offering good film formation but raising VOC concerns.</p><p>2. **Water-borne** coatings utilize water as a solvent, providing environmental benefits and lower emissions.</p><p>3. **High Solids** coatings contain a higher percentage of solid content, improving durability and reducing VOC levels.</p><p>4. **Powder** coatings are applied as dry powder and cured with heat, offering a durable, eco-friendly finish with minimal waste.</p></p>
