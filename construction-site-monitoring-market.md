<p><ul><li>Mobile Monitoring System</li><li>Fixed-point Monitoring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Construction site monitoring encompasses two primary market types: mobile monitoring systems and fixed-point monitoring systems. Mobile monitoring systems utilize portable devices to gather data from various locations on a site, offering flexibility and real-time updates. In contrast, fixed-point monitoring systems involve stationary sensors placed at specific locations to continuously track structural integrity, environmental conditions, and safety factors. Both systems enhance risk management, efficiency, and compliance on construction sites, catering to diverse project requirements and monitoring needs.</p></p>
