<p><p>The detergent grade enzymes market is categorized into four main types: protease, amylase, lipase, and cellulase. </p><p>Protease is used for breaking down proteins, making it effective in removing stains from biological origins. Amylase targets starches, aiding in the breakdown of carbohydrate-based stains. Lipase is responsible for breaking down fats and oils, ideal for greasy stains. Cellulase helps in maintaining fabric integrity while removing dirt, enhancing the cleaning performance and ensuring the longevity of textiles. Together, they enhance detergent efficacy.</p></p>
