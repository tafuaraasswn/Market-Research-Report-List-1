<p><strong>The Biometrics Authentication Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hand Based Recognition System</li><li>Eye Based Recognition System</li><li>Vein Pattern Based Recognition System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biometrics authentication market includes various technologies for identity verification based on unique physical characteristics. Hand-based recognition systems utilize fingerprints and palm prints for identification, offering high accuracy and ease of integration. Eye-based recognition systems, such as iris and retina scans, provide secure authentication through unique patterns in the eye. Vein pattern-based recognition systems analyze the unique patterns in an individual’s veins, often in the hand or finger, providing a highly secure option due to its difficulty to replicate. Each type serves distinct security needs across various industries.</p></p>
