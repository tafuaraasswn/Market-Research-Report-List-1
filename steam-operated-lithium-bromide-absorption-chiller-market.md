The Double Effect chiller utilizes two stages of heat exchange, improving efficiency and cooling capacity, making it suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, the Single Effect chiller operates on a single stage, offering a simpler design and lower initial costs, ideal for smaller systems. Both types leverage steam as a heat source, promoting energy efficiency.</p></p>
