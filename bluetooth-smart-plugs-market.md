<p><p>Bluetooth smart plugs come in various types, primarily including two-hole plugs, three-hole plugs, and porous plugs. Two-hole plugs are typically used for lightweight devices and offer basic functionality. Three-hole plugs provide an additional grounding feature, making them suitable for heavier appliances. Porous plugs are designed with multiple sockets, allowing multiple devices to be connected simultaneously. Each type facilitates remote control and automation through Bluetooth technology, enhancing energy efficiency and convenience in home or office settings.</p></p>
