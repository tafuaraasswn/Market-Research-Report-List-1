<p><strong>The Pallet Pooling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tray static leasing</li><li>Tray dynamic flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pallet pooling encompasses two main market types: tray static leasing and tray dynamic flow. In tray static leasing, companies rent pallets for a fixed period, maintaining control over their inventory and reducing management complexities. Conversely, tray dynamic flow involves a more flexible system where pallets are exchanged throughout the supply chain, optimizing utilization and minimizing costs. Both models aim to enhance logistical efficiency, streamline operations, and reduce waste, catering to different business needs in the pallet pooling landscape.</p></p>
