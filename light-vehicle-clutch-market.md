<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Clutch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Friction Clutch</li><li>Dry Friction Clutch</li><li>Electromagnetic Clutch</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle clutch market encompasses various types, including wet friction clutches, which use oil to enhance cooling and reduce wear, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Dry friction clutches operate without lubrication, providing direct engagement and are simpler in design, commonly used in most passenger vehicles. Electromagnetic clutches utilize electromagnetic force for engagement, offering precision and control, particularly in automatic systems. The "Others" category includes specialized clutches like centrifugal and spring-loaded variants, catering to niche applications.</p></p>
