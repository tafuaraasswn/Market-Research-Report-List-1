<p><p>The cargo plane lighting market comprises three main types: LED, halogen, and xenon lights. LED lights are energy-efficient, offer long lifespans, and provide bright illumination, making them increasingly popular in modern aviation. Halogen lights are known for their higher intensity and color accuracy but consume more energy and have shorter lifespans. Xenon lights deliver a powerful, white light and have a longer life than halogen, yet they can be costlier. Each type caters to specific operational and budgetary needs in cargo aviation.</p></p>
