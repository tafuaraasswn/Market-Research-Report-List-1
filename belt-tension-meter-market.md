<p><p>The belt tension meter market encompasses various types of belts based on their design and application. V-belts are widely used for power transmission in machinery due to their wedge-shaped profile, providing a strong grip. Flat belts, characterized by their rectangular cross-section, are commonly utilized in conveyor systems and simple drive applications. Round belts offer flexibility and are often used in applications requiring tight turns, while ribbed belts, with their grooves, enhance grip and reduce slippage in automotive and industrial settings.</p></p>
