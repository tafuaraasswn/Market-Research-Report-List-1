<p><p>The hemodialysis consumables market comprises essential components used in the dialysis process. Dialyzers, or artificial kidneys, filter toxins from the blood. Dialysis solutions, consisting of electrolytes and fluids, help maintain proper chemical balance during treatment. Hemodialysis catheters provide access to blood vessels, facilitating the flow of blood to and from the machine. AV fistula needles are specialized tools used to puncture the arteriovenous fistula for blood access. Other consumables include syringes, tubing, and disinfectants necessary for safe procedures.</p></p>
