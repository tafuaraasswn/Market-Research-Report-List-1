<p><p>Cloud engineering encompasses three main market types: Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS), Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS), and Software-as-a-Service (SaaS). IaaS provides virtualized computing resources over the internet, allowing businesses to rent infrastructure such as servers and storage. PaaS offers a development platform where developers can build, test, and deploy applications without managing underlying hardware. SaaS delivers software applications through the cloud, enabling users to access them via the internet without installation. Each model serves distinct needs in cloud computing.</p></p>
