<p><strong>The Automotive TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) market comprises two main types: uni-polar and bi-polar TVS devices. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to clamp positive voltage transients, making them suitable for applications with a consistent ground reference. In contrast, bi-polar TVS devices can handle both positive and negative voltage spikes, offering greater protection in applications where signal levels may fluctuate around a zero voltage reference. Both types are essential for safeguarding automotive electronics from voltage surges and ensuring reliable operation.</p></p>
