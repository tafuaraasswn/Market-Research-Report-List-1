<p><p>The Industrial Duct Exhaust Muffler market includes various types designed to reduce noise generated by exhaust systems. Resistive mufflers use sound-absorbing materials to minimize sound levels, while resistant mufflers utilize their structure to obstruct sound waves. Impedance composite mufflers combine different materials for improved performance. Micro perforated plate mufflers feature tiny holes for sound reduction, and small hole mufflers use narrow openings to disrupt noise paths. Active mufflers employ electronic controls to counteract sound, enhancing noise reduction efficiency.</p></p>
