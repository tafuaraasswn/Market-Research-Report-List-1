<p><p>The dewatering equipment market includes various types designed for solid-liquid separation. Centrifuges use high rotational speeds to separate solids from liquids. Belt filter presses utilize conveyor belts to compress sludge and extract water. Vacuum filters employ suction to draw moisture from sludges, while drying beds rely on natural evaporation and drainage. Sludge lagoons store and naturally solidify waste over time. Other types encompass innovations and hybrid technologies that enhance dewatering efficiency and adaptability for various applications.</p></p>
