<p><p>The textile enzymes market includes several key types: **Pectinase** breaks down pectin in plant fibers, enhancing the quality of textiles. **Cellulase** strengthens cotton fibers, improving softness and dye uptake. **Amylase** assists in starch removal during fabric processing. **Laccase** aids in dyeing and bleaching by oxidizing phenolic compounds. **Catalase** helps in the removal of hydrogen peroxide, ensuring color retention. Other enzymes cater to specific processes, enhancing efficiency and sustainability in textile manufacturing. Together, they drive innovation and environmental responsibility in the industry.</p></p>
