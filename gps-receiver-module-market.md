<p><p>A GPS Receiver Module is a device that receives signals from Global Positioning System satellites to determine its precise location, speed, and time. The rise of smart cities and advancements in transportation infrastructure are further propelling market growth, as governments and enterprises seek better navigation solutions. Additionally, the proliferation of smartphones and wearable technology has significantly contributed to the adoption of GPS modules, enhancing user experience with real-time location tracking.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift towards miniaturization and integration of GPS modules with other technologies, such as cellular, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth, to provide enhanced functionality. Furthermore, the incorporation of advanced features like Assisted GPS (A-GPS) and Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) compatibility is becoming standard, ensuring greater accuracy and reliability. As autonomous vehicles and drones come to the forefront, the demand for sophisticated GPS solutions is set to escalate, marking a promising trajectory for the market.</p></p>
